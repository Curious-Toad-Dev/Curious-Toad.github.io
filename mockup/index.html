Curious Toad Branding Mockups

Here are some very rough initial branding ideas. I literally threw the ideas down in an hour to get a feel for things... without even so much as a grid.

This was designed for larger screens. Please view on desktop as it will not look right on mobile.

Intro

The main concept is a visual representation of my skillset and interests: I enjoy both the creative and technical aspects of projects. For as long as I can remember, I've instinctively found the patterns in everyday things - the shapes of a shadow on a wall, the patterns found/made in nature.

The blueprint idea plays upon that; analysing and finding the technical details/structure/underpinnings behind something naturally creative or beautiful.

The choice of a butterfly for the hero image was for many reasons (visually striking, there's a huge variety of species, graphically ornate), but overridingly as a tribute to my daughter's experience: her favourite book is The Very Hungry Caterpillar, which we read to her everyday in hospital - it felt like she had her own metamorphosis as well.

Note: This is all conceptual and needs refinement - it was just to get an idea for layout and colourways. For example, typography is at browser defaults, elements are not spaced correctly etc.
